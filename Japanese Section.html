I have currently taught myself the two basic scripts that Japanese has, Katakana (カタカナ)
and Hiragana (ひらがな).

At the moment, I am currently working on the kanji (漢字), which is Japanese's largest script.

There are 2136 Kanji used commonly, and I currently have the writing of around 700 memorized.

After I learn their writings, I will apply pronounciation, and then work on grammer and vocabulary from there.
